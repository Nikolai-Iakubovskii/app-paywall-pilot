3. Adapty "50+ experiments = 18.7x revenue" — correlation not causation; vendor_blog Operator Insight
4. Apphud "80% Day 1 trial conversion" — no sample-size disclosure
5. Decoy effect (Ariely Economist study) — applicability to mobile paywalls not isolated; treat as Operator Insight when applied
6. Multi-page vs single-page paywalls — conflicting results, kept as Hypothesis
7. RevenueFlo "16pt pricing minimum" — observed correlation, not Apple-published rule
